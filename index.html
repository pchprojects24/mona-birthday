Three cats with three very distinct takes on life — but united in their appreciation for warm laps, window sills, and the occasional treat.

— Marice & Chad
Proud staff to three very opinionated cats

Alice

Alice arrived first, setting the tone for dignified chaos. She tolerates the others with the patience of a queen and expects nothing less than absolute deference from everyone in the household.

Olive

Olive is the reason cabinet locks were invented. She is relentlessly curious and completely unrepentant about it. If something can be opened, knocked over, or thoroughly investigated, Olive is on it.

Beatrice

Beatrice came last and made the biggest entrance. She remains the loudest presence in any room she graces, and has strong opinions about dinner timing that she will share at volume.
